# Instrument Cluster

![Diagram](./8W-40-4.jpg)

**Notes:** Diagram shows instrument cluster illumination circuit with two parallel paths: one through headlamp switch (park/head positions) and another direct feed through Joint Connector No. 5. Instrument cluster contains seven indicator bulbs. Circuit B1 shown as PARK/HEAD with reference to 8W-50-2.
