# GENERAL INFORMATION - DESCRIPTION AND OPERATION (Continued)

![Diagram](./8W-01-6.jpg)

**Notes:** This page contains general safety warnings and diagnostic information rather than a wiring diagram. Key topics include: (1) Warnings about hot parts, flames/sparks near battery, and jumper wire usage; (2) Electrostatic discharge (ESD) handling procedures for sensitive devices; (3) Diagnosis and testing guidance including use of voltmeter with 10-megohm or greater impedance; (4) Troubleshooting tools including test light, jumper wire, and probing tools; (5) Information about intermittent and poor connections caused by faulty electrical connections or wiring. The page includes an Electrostatic Discharge Symbol (Figure 4, J04W-193) showing a hand with prohibition symbol. Instructions emphasize proper grounding procedures, avoiding jumper wires across loads between battery feed and ground, and proper connector seating and terminal engagement.
