# Overhead Console - Visor/Vanity Lamps

![Diagram](./8W-40-5.jpg)

**Notes:** Diagram shows overhead console visor/vanity lamp circuit. Power feed M1 splits to left and right vanity lamps via S326. Ground return Z1 from both lamps combines at S323 and routes through Junction Block to ground G201.
