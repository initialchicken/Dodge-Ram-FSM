# SERVICE PROCEDURES (Continued) - TERMINAL/CONNECTOR REPAIR-MOLEX CONNECTORS

![Diagram](./8W-01-11.jpg)

**Notes:** This is a service procedure page (8W-01 GENERAL INFORMATION) showing repair procedures for Molex, Thomas and Betts connectors. Includes instructions for: (1) Disconnecting battery, (2) Disconnecting connector from mating half, (3) Inserting terminal releasing special tool 6742, (4) Using special tool 6742 to release locking fingers, (5) Pulling wire to remove from connector, (6) Repairing or replacing connector/terminal, (7) Pushing in two lock tabs, (8) Inserting probe end of special tool 6934, (9) Grasping wire and tool 6934 to slowly remove wire and terminal, (10) Repairing or replacing terminal, (11) Installing wire and terminal, (12) Fully seating terminal, (13) Pushing in single lock tab. Special tools referenced: 6742 (T&BW-234), 6934, T&BW-235. Also includes CONNECTOR REPLACEMENT procedure for disconnecting battery, removing connector locking wedges if required (Fig. 15).
