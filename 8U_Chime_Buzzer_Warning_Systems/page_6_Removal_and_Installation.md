# 8U-6 CHIME/BUZZER WARNING SYSTEMS

## REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION

### CHIME WARNING SYSTEM SWITCHES

Service procedures for the various hard-wired switches used in the chime warning system can be found in the Removal and Installation section of the proper group, as follows:

- Driver door jamb switch - refer to Group 8Q - Vehicle Theft/Security Systems
- Driver seat belt switch - refer to Group 23 - Body
- Headlamp switch - refer to Group 8E - Instrument Panel Systems
- Key-in ignition switch - refer to Group 8D - Ignition Systems.
