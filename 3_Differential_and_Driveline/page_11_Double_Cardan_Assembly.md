# DIFFERENTIAL AND DRIVELINE 3-11

## DISASSEMBLY AND ASSEMBLY (Continued)

### ASSEMBLY

During assembly, ensure that the alignment marks on the link yoke and propeller shaft yoke are aligned.

1. Apply extreme pressure (EP) N.L.G.I. Grade 1 or 2 grease to inside of yoke bores to aid in installation.

2. Fit a cross into the propeller shaft yoke (Fig. 24).

*Fig. 24 Install Cross In Yoke*

3. Place a bearing cap over the trunnion and align the cap with the yoke bore (Fig. 25). Keep the needle bearings upright in the bearing assembly. A needle bearing lying at the bottom of the cap will prevent proper assembly.

*Fig. 25 Install Bearing Cap*

4. Press the bearing cap into the yoke bore enough to install a snap ring (Fig. 26).

5. Install a snap ring.

*Fig. 26 Press In Bearing Cap*

6. Flip the propeller shaft yoke and install the bearing cap onto the opposite trunnion. Install a snap ring (Fig. 27).

*Fig. 27 Press In Bearing Cap*

7. Fit the link yoke on the remaining two trunnions and press both bearing caps into place (Fig. 28).

8. Install snap rings.
