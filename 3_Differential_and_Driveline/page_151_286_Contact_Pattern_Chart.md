# DIFFERENTIAL AND DRIVELINE 3-151

## ADJUSTMENTS (Continued)

### GEAR TOOTH CONTACT PATTERNS

[Figure: Fig. 63 Gear Tooth Contact Patterns]

| DRIVE SIDE OF RING GEAR TEETH | COAST SIDE OF RING GEAR TEETH | CONDITION/CORRECTION |
|-------------------------------|-------------------------------|----------------------|
| [Pattern centered on tooth] | [Pattern centered, slightly toward toe] | **DESIRABLE CONTACT PATTERN.** Pattern should be centered on the drive side of tooth. Pattern should be centered on the coast side of tooth, but may be slightly toward the toe. There should always be some clearance between contact pattern and top of the tooth. |
| [Pattern toward toe] | [Pattern toward toe] | Ring gear backlash correct. **THINNER PINION GEAR DEPTH SHIM REQUIRED.** |
| [Pattern toward heel] | [Pattern toward heel] | Ring gear backlash correct. **THICKER PINION GEAR DEPTH SHIM REQUIRED.** |
| [Pattern toward top of tooth] | [Pattern toward top of tooth] | Pinion gear depth shim correct. **DECREASE RING GEAR BACKLASH.** |
| [Pattern toward bottom of tooth] | [Pattern toward bottom of tooth] | Pinion gear depth shim correct. **INCREASE RING GEAR BACKLASH.** |

**Tooth Orientation:**
- HEEL — TOE — TOE — HEEL (across tooth face)
