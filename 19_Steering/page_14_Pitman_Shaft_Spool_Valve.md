## SPOOL VALVE

### DISASSEMBLY

(1) Remove lock nut (Fig. 10).

(2) Remove adjuster nut with Spanner Wrench C-4381.

(3) Remove thrust support assembly out of the housing (Fig. 11).

(4) Pull stub shaft and valve assembly from the housing (Fig. 12).
