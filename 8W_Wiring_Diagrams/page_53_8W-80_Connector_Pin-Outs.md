**ENGINE COOLANT TEMPERATURE SENSOR**

| CAV | CIRCUIT | FUNCTION |
|-----|---------|----------|

| B | K2 18TN/BK | ENGINE COOLANT TEMPERATURE SENSOR SIGNAL |
