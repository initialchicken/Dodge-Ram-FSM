## DISASSEMBLY AND ASSEMBLY (Continued)

(21) Remove intermediate shaft thrust washer. Triangular shaped washer will either be on shaft pilot hub or in rear clutch retainer (Fig. 110).

(22) Remove thrust plate from intermediate shaft (Fig. 111).

(23) Remove intermediate shaft-planetary geartrain assembly (Fig. 112).

(24) If overdrive unit is not to be serviced, install Alignment Shaft 6227-2 into the overdrive unit to prevent disassembly of the unit. This is necessary during service of main transmission components.

(25) Loosen rear band adjust and loosen adjusting screw 3-4 turns.

(26) Remove snap ring that retains low-reverse drum to overdrive piston retainer hub (Fig. 113).
