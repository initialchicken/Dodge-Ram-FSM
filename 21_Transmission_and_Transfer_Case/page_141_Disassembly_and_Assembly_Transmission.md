## TRANSMISSION

### DISASSEMBLY

(1) Clean transmission exterior with steam gun or with solvent. Wear eye protection during cleaning operations.

(2) Place transmission in a vertical position.

(3) Measure and record input shaft end play readings.

(4) Remove shift and throttle levers from valve body manual lever shaft.

(5) Place transmission in a horizontal position.

(6) Remove oil pan, filter and gasket.

(7) Remove filter from valve body (Fig. 87). Keep filling screws separate from other valve body screws. Filler screws are longer and should be kept with filter.
