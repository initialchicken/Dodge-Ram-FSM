(18) Remove the six (6) accelerator pedal position sensor assy.-to-cylinder head bracket bolts (Fig. 69) and secure the entire assembly out of the way. Disconnect the APPS connector (Fig. 70). It is not necessary to disconnect the cables from the throttle control assy. Refer to Group 14, Fuel Systems for applicable cautions and warnings.

(20) Remove engine oil level indicator tube attaching bolt from the air inlet housing.

(21) Remove the charge air cooler-to-air inlet housing pipe.

(22) Remove the air inlet housing and intake grid heater from the intake manifold cover.

(23) Remove the engine lift bracket from the rear of the cylinder head.
