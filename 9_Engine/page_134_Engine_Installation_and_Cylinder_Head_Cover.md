### CYLINDER HEAD COVER

Die-cast magnesium cylinder head covers (Fig. 14) reduce noise and provide a good sealing surface. A steel backed silicon gasket is used with the cylinder head cover (Fig. 15).

#### REMOVAL

(1) Disconnect the negative cable from the battery.
(2) Disconnect closed ventilation system and evaporation control system from cylinder head cover. Identify each system for installation.
(3) Remove the upper intake manifold to remove the right side head cover (refer to Group 11, Exhaust System and Intake Manifold).
(4) Remove cylinder head cover bolts and stud bolts. Remove the covers and gaskets. The gasket may be used again.

#### INSTALLATION

(1) Clean cylinder head cover gasket surface.
(2) Clean head rail, if necessary.
(3) Check the gasket for use in head cover installation. If damaged, use a new gasket.
(4) Install the gasket onto the head rail. For the left side the number tab is at the front of engine with the number up. For the right side the number tab is at the rear of engine with the number up.
