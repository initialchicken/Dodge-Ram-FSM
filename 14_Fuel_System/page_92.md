The overflow valve (pressure relief valve) is located at the outside of fuel injection pump (Fig. 62). It connects the fuel return line (banjo fitting) to the pump. The valve has no internal serviceable parts and must be replaced as an assembly. Two sealing gaskets are used. One gasket is located between pump and banjo fitting. The other is located between the banjo fitting and end of valve.
