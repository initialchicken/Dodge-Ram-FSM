### FUEL INJECTION PUMP TIMING

With the Bosch VP44 injection pump, there are no mechanical adjustments needed for fuel injection timing. All timing and fuel adjustments are made by the Engine Control Module (ECM). However, if a Diagnostic Trouble Code (DTC) has been stored indicating an "engine sync error" or a "static timing error", perform the following.

**Note:** If this DTC appears after installation of a new or rebuilt injection pump, the pump keyway has probably been installed backwards. Refer to Fuel Injection Pump Removal/Installation for keyway information.

(1) Remove plastic access cover, injection pump nut and washer (Fig. 33). Locate keyway behind washer.
