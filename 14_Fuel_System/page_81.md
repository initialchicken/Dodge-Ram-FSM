(7) Perform previous air bleeding procedure steps using fuel transfer pump. Be sure fuel is present at fuel supply line (Fig. 37) before proceeding. (8) Crank the engine for 30 seconds at a time to allow air trapped in the injection pump to vent out the drain manifold.
