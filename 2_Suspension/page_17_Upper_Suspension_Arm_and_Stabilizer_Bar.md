### STABILIZER BAR

#### REMOVAL

1. Raise and support the vehicle.

2. Hold the link shafts with a wrench and remove the nuts (Fig. 7).

3. Remove the retainers and grommets from the stabilizer bar links.

4. Remove the stabilizer bar link cotter pins and nuts from the axle brackets.

5. Remove the links from the axle brackets with Puller C-3894-A (Fig. 8).

6. Remove the stabilizer bar clamps from the frame rails and remove the stabilizer bar.

*Fig. 7 Stabilizer Bar*

*Fig. 8 Stabilizer Link*

#### INSTALLATION

1. Position the stabilizer bar on the frame rail and install the clamps and bolts. Ensure the bar is centered with equal spacing on both sides. Tighten the bolts to 54 N·m (40 ft. lbs.).

2. Install links to the axle bracket and tighten nut to 68 N·m (50 ft. lbs.).

3. Install links, retainers, grommets and nuts to the stabilizer bar (Fig. 7). Hold the link shaft with a wrench and tighten the nuts to 37 N·m (27 ft. lbs.).

4. Remove the supports and lower the vehicle.
